At PatienTrials, our laser focus on putting Patients First in Clinical Trials, brings in Patient Centricity, improving Patient Recruitment, Patient Adherence and Patient Retention, thus reducing cycle time in over all drug development. PatienTrials built on touch points, empowers patients with treatment information at their fingertips on a familiar instant messaging spo compliant platform.
