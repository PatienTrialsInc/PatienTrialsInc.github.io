ABOUT PATIENTRIALS
Patient Engagement Platform for clinical trials, empowers clinicians and sponsors to run global clinical trials virtually or through selected sites, more efficiently in a closed community setting over HIPAA & GDPR compliant collaboration platform. Providing patients with the information on progress of their treatment in a community setting only promotes self care. PatienTrials captures high-quality patient generated data through solutions based on instant messaging, AI and bots, is poised to make a positive impact on over all drug development.

An unified interface powered by PatienTrials, empowers clinicians and site staff to run multiple clinical trials in parallel, across different sponsors. On the other hand, sponsors have full visibility on sites performance and patients engagement across their studies. Monitored by proprietary AI agent for adverse events, PatienTrials reduces study risk and lowers overall drug development costs. Founded in 2017, PatienTrials is the first company to bring clinical trials on to a HIPAA & GDPR compliant instant messaging platform.
